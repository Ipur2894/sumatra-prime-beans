About Us

We provide high-quality Arabica Gayo green coffee beans directly sourced from trusted farmers in the Gayo Highlands of Aceh, Sumatra. Our beans are available by pre-order and ideal for importers, roasters, and coffee enthusiasts worldwide. We help connect international buyers with Indonesia’s finest coffee origin through a direct sourcing approach.
